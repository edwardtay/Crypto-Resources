# USE crypto #
 What would you do with your crypto?

1. [**Earn**](#1-earn) ‚Äî do something of value to get additional money.
2. [**Hold**](#2-hold) ‚Äî custody and secure your money for saving.
3. [**Borrow / Lend**](#3-borrow--lend) ‚Äî pay funds to borrow money from protocols and banks.
4. [**Trade**](#4-trade) ‚Äî exchange your money for something of value.
5. [**Bet**](#5-bet) ‚Äî risk and earn money by making a bet (shorting/longing included).
